The Best Movies According to Putra

My top 3 Movies

Spider-Man : Into the Spider-Verse (2018)

"Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" is a visually stunning and emotionally resonant animated superhero film that breathes new life into the Spider-Man franchise with its unique animation style, diverse characters, and engaging storytelling.

Your Name (2016)

"Your Name" is a captivating blend of breathtaking animation, a heartfelt love story, and unexpected supernatural twists that will leave you both mesmerized and emotionally moved.

The Batman (2022)

"The Batman" delivers a dark and gripping noir-inspired take on the iconic character, with Robert Pattinson's brooding performance and a compelling narrative making it a must-see for fans of the Caped Crusader.
